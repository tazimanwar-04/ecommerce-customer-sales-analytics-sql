##### **1. Revenue Growth \& Seasonality Insight:**



###### Finding

Total revenue: 13.59M

Revenue grew rapidly throughout 2017.

Peak month: November 2017 (~1.01M).

Revenue stabilized between 850K–1M per month in 2018.



###### Interpretation

The business experienced strong scaling in 2017 and reached operational maturity in 2018.

The November spike suggests seasonal promotions such as Black Friday significantly impact sales.



###### Business Recommendation

Increase marketing and inventory allocation before Q4.

Use historical seasonality to forecast demand.

Optimize promotional strategies during peak months.





##### **2. Category Revenue Concentration Insight**



###### Finding

Top revenue-generating categories:

Beauty \& Health

Watches \& Gifts

Home \& Bedding

Sports \& Leisure

Computer Accessories



###### Interpretation

Revenue is diversified across lifestyle and home segments rather than being electronics-dominated.

The platform has strong positioning in personal care and home-related categories.



###### Business Recommendation

Prioritize marketing spend on top 5 categories.

Bundle complementary products (e.g., home décor + bedding).

Improve categorization for products with missing category labels.





##### **3.Customer Retention Insight (Critical Finding)**



###### Finding

Unique customers: ~96K

Repeat customers: ~3%

Majority are single-purchase buyers.



###### Interpretation

The platform heavily relies on customer acquisition rather than retention.

Low repeat rate suggests limited loyalty or engagement.



###### Business Recommendation

Introduce loyalty programs.

Offer discount incentives for second purchase.

Launch targeted email campaigns for previous buyers.

Implement subscription or rewards models.







##### **4. Geographic Revenue Concentration Insight**



###### Finding

São Paulo (SP) generates ~5.2M revenue.

SP alone contributes nearly 38% of total revenue.

Revenue highly concentrated in southeastern Brazil.



###### Interpretation

The business is geographically dependent on a single major state.

This concentration creates expansion opportunities but also risk.



###### Business Recommendation

Expand targeted marketing in underperforming states.

Improve logistics in northern and northeastern regions.

Develop region-specific campaigns.







##### **5. Logistics \& Delivery Performance Insight**



###### Finding

Average delivery: 11.88 days earlier than estimated

High percentage of delivered orders (~97%)



###### Interpretation

Delivery estimation is conservative.

Logistics performance is strong and reliable.



###### Business Recommendation

Reduce buffer in estimated delivery dates.

Market faster-than-expected delivery as a competitive advantage.

Optimize customer communication around delivery expectations.





##### **6. Product Revenue Distribution Insight (Advanced)**



###### Finding

No single product dominates revenue.

Top product contributes only ~64K compared to total 13.6M.



###### Interpretation

Revenue is well-distributed across catalog.

Business risk from product dependency is low.



###### Business Recommendation

Maintain diversified catalog strategy.

Promote mid-performing products to increase basket size.
